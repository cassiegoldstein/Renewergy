Frequently Asked Questions


What is the difference between solar thermal and solar PV?

Renewergy sells a range of solar thermal and solar PV products. The primary difference between the two is that solar PV panels product electricity, while solar thermal system product heat. Our solar PV's can cover around 60% of your household's energy needs, while our solar thermal systems can convert 90% of solar rays into usable energy!

Does Renewergy provide installation services?

Yes! Renewergy has a team of dedicated electricians and roofers who are on-hand to install any and all of our renewable energy products. With a purchase of $400+, installation is free within 50 miles!

Is Renewergy affordable?

Not only are Renewergy products eco-friendly, but they're wallet-friendly! Renewergy is committed to providing the lowest-cost, highest-quality solar energy products on the market. Price match guaranteed.

Can I sell energy back to my utility company?

Yes! As long as your house is connected to the national grid, you can sell any surplus electricity back to your energy supplier.

Do your solar panel systems need annual maintainence?

Renewergy's solar panels are built to last! We suggest cleaning them every six months, and an annual check is recommended.
